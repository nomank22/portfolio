Hey everyone! I’m Noman Khan (I also go by Nomi, pronounced “NAWmi”), and I’m currently a first-year graduate student at the University of Michigan School of Information (UMSI) located in Ann Arbor! I’m hoping to specialize in UX Research and Design/Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). I was born and raised in Lawrenceville, NJ (near Princeton University), so moving to Ann Arbor this past Fall was definitely a new experience.

In my free time, I like to watch Youtube, play video games with my friends on my PC or Nintendo Switch, and sometimes stream my gaming experience on Twitch.tv, which is a live-streaming platform that allows users to stream gaming/variety content and interact with viewers via live chat. It’s been a long time since I’ve streamed since I’ve been busy with school, but I’m hoping to get back into it this Summer!

Here is my beautiful family! I’ve lived with my aunt and uncle ever since 1st grade due to my parents being deported, so they’re almost my 2nd pair of parents. I also grew up with my sister and 2 cousins, who are pretty much my siblings :).
