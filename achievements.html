What's an achievement?

To me, an achievment is something that shows dedication, hard work, and showcases the talent and drive of an individual.

Why choose to talk about achievements?

As many others, I noticed I found myself suffering from Imposter Syndrome. For those who don't know, Imposter Syndrome can be defined as "defined as doubting your abilities and feeling like a fraud. It disproportionately affects high-achieving people, who find it difficult to accept their accomplishments. Many question whether they're deserving of accolades"
